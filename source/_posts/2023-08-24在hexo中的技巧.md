# 主题篇

## 1. 本地化搭建

修改文件 `_config.icarus.yml`

```yaml
providers:
    cdn: '/assets/cdn/${filename}'
    fontcdn: '/assets/fontcdn/${type}?family=${fontname}'
    iconcdn: '/assets/iconcdn/all.css'
```

[assets.zip][1]

将静态资源放入`/source/assets` 没有就新建`assets`
